============================================================
SECTION: CENTRAL AGRICULTURE SCHEMES – GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
============================================================

SCHEME_NAME: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
TYPE: Income Support
APPLICABLE_STATES: All States and Union Territories
APPLICABLE_CROPS: All Crops
BENEFIT: ₹6000 per year in 3 installments
ELIGIBILITY: Small and marginal farmers owning cultivable land
DESCRIPTION:
Provides direct income support to farmers to supplement financial needs for agricultural and domestic expenses.
OFFICIAL_PORTAL: https://pmkisan.gov.in
------------------------------------------------------------

SCHEME_NAME: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
TYPE: Crop Insurance
APPLICABLE_STATES: All States (State Participation Based)
APPLICABLE_CROPS: Notified Crops by State
PREMIUM_RATES:
Kharif Crops: 2%
Rabi Crops: 1.5%
Commercial/Horticulture Crops: 5%
COVERAGE:
Natural Calamities, Pest Attack, Crop Loss, Prevented Sowing
DESCRIPTION:
Provides financial support to farmers suffering crop loss or damage due to unforeseen events.
------------------------------------------------------------

SCHEME_NAME: Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
TYPE: Agricultural Loan
APPLICABLE_STATES: All States
BENEFIT:
Short-term credit up to ₹3 lakh
Interest Subvention available
DESCRIPTION:
Provides farmers easy access to credit for crop production and allied activities.
------------------------------------------------------------

SCHEME_NAME: Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)
TYPE: Irrigation & Water Management
BENEFIT:
Subsidy for drip irrigation, sprinkler systems, water conservation structures
DESCRIPTION:
Aims to improve water use efficiency and expand irrigation coverage.
------------------------------------------------------------

SCHEME_NAME: National Food Security Mission (NFSM)
TYPE: Crop Productivity Enhancement
TARGET_CROPS:
Rice, Wheat, Pulses, Coarse Cereals, Oilseeds
DESCRIPTION:
Improves productivity through seed distribution, training, and subsidies.
------------------------------------------------------------

SCHEME_NAME: Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)
TYPE: Organic Farming Promotion
BENEFIT:
Financial assistance for organic inputs and certification
DESCRIPTION:
Encourages cluster-based organic farming practices.
------------------------------------------------------------

SCHEME_NAME: Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)
TYPE: Infrastructure Loan
BENEFIT:
Interest subvention and credit guarantee support
DESCRIPTION:
Supports post-harvest infrastructure and community farming assets.
------------------------------------------------------------

SCHEME_NAME: e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)
TYPE: Digital Market Platform
BENEFIT:
Online trading, better price discovery
DESCRIPTION:
Integrates APMC markets into unified digital platform.
------------------------------------------------------------
